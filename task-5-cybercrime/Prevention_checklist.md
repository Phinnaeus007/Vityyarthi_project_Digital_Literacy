ADDITIONAL SAFETY MEASURES:

- Always check for “https://” and a lock symbol in websites
- Do not save passwords on shared or public devices
- Regularly update your passwords
- Avoid downloading apps from unknown sources
- Check app permissions before installing
- Use official apps for banking and payments
- Enable account alerts for transactions
- Review bank statements regularly for suspicious activity
- Do not respond to messages asking for urgent action without verification
- Cross-check information by contacting official customer support
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes
- Use a secure network connection whenever possible
- Backup important data regularly
- Educate yourself about common online scams
- Report suspicious messages immediately

UPI / ADVANCED SAFETY:
- Remember: receiving money does NOT require a PIN
- Do not accept collect requests from unknown users
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money

GENERAL HABITS:
- Log out from accounts after use on shared devices
- Do not auto-save card details on websites
- Be cautious of “too good to be true” offers
